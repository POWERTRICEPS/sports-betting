### Feedback we want for our product

- User preferences and ideas on the color scheme and UI design to make the web app look more official. One specific metric to measure improvement is the individual game page's load time, often taking 30 seconds.
- User preference on whether they want more filter options, prop options (Blocks, Steals, Etc), or game stats on our live NBA games. One specific metric for improvement is to measure how intuitive it is for a user to find Austin Reaves' Projected Assists using our filter system, or how intuitive it is to find the Rebound Leader in a certain game.
- User satisfaction with our win probability model's accuracy based on live game data. One specific metric for improvement is the average difference of our model from credible win probability models such as ESPN across live NBA games.
